Dmitri Mendeleev

Creator of the far-sighted Periodic Table

A brief timeline:

  • 1834 - Born in Verkhnie Aremzyani, Russian Empire
  • 1849 - Applied to Moscow University, but was rejected
  • 1850 - Entered the Main Pedagogical Institute
  • 1849 - Published a textbook Organic Chemistry, which would go on to win the Demidov Prize of the Petersburg Academy of Sciences
